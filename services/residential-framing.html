Residential Framing

The backbone of every great home — precision framing done right the first time, so everything built on top of it stands strong for decades.

The Foundation of a Great Build

Framing is one of the most critical phases of any home construction project. It determines the structural integrity, layout, and longevity of everything that follows. At Top Notch Builders, we treat framing with the precision and care it deserves — because a well-framed home makes every other trade's job easier and your home stronger.

Our crew has framed everything from single-family homes to complex multi-level builds across the Cedar Rapids and Iowa City area. We work efficiently without cutting corners, keeping your project on schedule and on budget.

What We Frame

  • New Home ConstructionFull framing packages for new single-family homes from the ground up.
  • Custom Floor PlansWe work from your architectural plans and bring complex designs to life accurately.
  • Multi-Level HomesTwo-story, split-level, and vaulted ceiling framing handled with expertise.
  • Garages & OutbuildingsAttached and detached garages, shops, and storage structures.
  • Roof FramingHip, gable, shed, and complex roof systems framed to code.
  • Structural ModificationsLoad-bearing wall removals, beam installations, and structural repairs.

Built for Iowa Weather

Iowa winters are no joke. We frame with that in mind — proper bracing, quality lumber, and attention to detail that keeps your home tight and energy-efficient year-round. Every frame we build is inspected and built to exceed local code requirements.
