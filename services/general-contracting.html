General Contracting

One trusted team to manage your build from blueprint to handoff — coordinating crews, schedules, and quality so you don't have to.

Built Right Everytime, Start to Finish

As your general contractor, Top Notch Builders of Iowa takes ownership of every phase of your project. From initial planning and permits to final walkthrough, we coordinate the trades, schedules, and material deliveries so your build stays on track and on budget.

Whether you're putting up a new commercial space or tackling a major residential project, our experience across Cedar Rapids, Iowa City, and the surrounding areas means we know what it takes to keep your project compliant, efficient, and built to last.

What's Included

  • Project Planning & EstimatingRealistic timelines and transparent quotes before work starts.
  • Permits & InspectionsWe handle the paperwork and code coordination so your build stays compliant.
  • Trade CoordinationFramers, electricians, plumbers, finishers — all scheduled and managed by us.
  • On-Site SupervisionDaily oversight to keep quality high and surprises low.
  • Materials ManagementPremium materials sourced and delivered on schedule.
  • Final WalkthroughWe don't call it done until you're happy with every detail.

Residential & Commercial

From new builds and major additions to commercial buildouts, we scale our process to your project. You'll work directly with the crew on your site — no middlemen, no surprises.
