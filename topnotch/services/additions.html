Additions

More space, more value, more home — built to blend seamlessly with what you already have and last just as long.

Expand Without Moving

Sometimes your home just needs more room. Whether your family is growing, you need a dedicated workspace, or you've always wanted that extra bedroom or sunroom — a well-built addition is the smartest investment you can make in your existing home.

At Top Notch Builders, we design and build additions that match your home's existing style so well you'd never know they weren't always there. We handle everything from the foundation to the finishing touches.

Types of Additions We Build

  • Room AdditionsBedrooms, home offices, playrooms — added square footage that fits your lifestyle.
  • Master Suite AdditionsPrivate retreats with en-suite bathrooms, walk-in closets, and custom finishes.
  • Sunrooms & Four-Season RoomsBright, comfortable spaces that let you enjoy the outdoors year-round.
  • Garage AdditionsAttached garages and garage expansions built to match your home's exterior.
  • Second Story AdditionsAdd an entire floor when lot space is limited but you need more room.
  • Mudrooms & EntrywaysFunctional additions that keep the rest of your home clean and organized.

Seamless Integration

The best additions are the ones you can't tell were added. We match rooflines, siding, trim, and interior finishes so your addition flows naturally from the existing structure. Every project is permitted, inspected, and built to code.
